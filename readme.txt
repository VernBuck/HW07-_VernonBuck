Welcome to space invaders. 
Your objective is to destroy all of the enemy ships by shooting them (pressing A)
In order to move simply click the left and right arrow keys (pressing <- or ->)
If the ships touch the bottom of the screen you lose
If you can reach a score of 20 you win (score is on the bottom right your score goes up by one per kill)
If your lives reach 0 then you lose (lives are shown on the bottom left getting hit by enemy bullet reduces lives by 1 per bullet)
To start to game from the title screen press START
To pause during the game press START
